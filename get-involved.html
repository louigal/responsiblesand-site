Get involved

We'd like to hear from you.

Three ways in

Join as a member

Structured participation in shared work. Help shape how responsible sourcing of sand and silicates develops in practice — from the inside, early.

Partner on a project

Co-develop a demonstration, a piece of targeted research, or a regional pilot. Doesn't require full membership to start.

Collaborate or advise

Standards bodies, policy processes, or lighter-touch engagement with the Coalition's work. We're open to conversations about what that looks like.

What membership involves

Membership is open to organisations that have a clear stake in how sand and silicates are sourced, used or governed; that support the Coalition's purpose; and that are willing to contribute constructively to shared work.

In practice, that means shared commitments to engagement, good practice and collective problem-solving. The details are set out in the membership agreement.

Fees are tiered by actor type and organisational size. The fee schedule is set out below.

Fee levels

Category Who it's for Fee (AUD)
Corporate — Large Multinational or major manufacturer / builder $75,000
Corporate — Medium Regional or national operator $35,000
Corporate — Small / SME Smaller company, innovator or niche supplier $15,000
NGO / Research institution Civil society, academic or research organisations $0 – $3,000
Government / Intergovernmental National ministries, development agencies, multilateral bodies $0 – $10,000

Fees are set by category and reviewed periodically with advice from the multi-stakeholder Council. If you have questions, we're happy to talk them through.

Ready to talk?

Whether you're thinking about membership, a project, or something else entirely — get in touch and we'll work out the best way forward together.
