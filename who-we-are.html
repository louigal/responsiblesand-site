Who we are

The Coalition for Responsible Sand and Silicates is a cross-sector platform. We connect companies, public-good actors and standard-setters to define responsibility, build practical guidance, and coordinate action.

Why join Get involved

What we stand for

We believe responsibility for sand and silicates should be defined with the same seriousness now expected in other mineral and materials supply chains.

  • Clarity — plain language, shared definitions, and practical tools.
  • Credibility — evidence-led approaches that hold up to scrutiny.
  • Collaboration — progress built through shared problem-solving and coordinated action.
  • Respect — reconnecting materials with the systems, places and people they affect.

What we do

The Coalition focuses on four areas:

  • Define responsibility — clear guidance, practical tools and shared red lines.
  • Demonstrate solutions — test approaches in real projects and supply chains.
  • Build shared intelligence — make data and insight usable.
  • Align existing efforts — work with standards and policy processes to fill gaps.

If you are exploring whether to join, the best starting point is our overview of what membership enables and what members can expect over 2026–2028.

Where we work

Our work is global. We engage across sectors that depend on sand and silicates — including construction, infrastructure, glass, electronics and manufacturing — and across the public policy and standards landscape shaping responsible sourcing.
