Where we are on the journey

This platform grew from the aspirations of our founding members and the insights of dozens of organizations who shaped the vision.

We launched after two years of co-design, including research for the OECD and working with the Global Centre for Mineral Security.

Key moments:

  • Asking the question — is it time to treat sand and silicates as part of responsible minerals supply? Research for the OECD confirmed these materials belong on international agendas and exposed major gaps in existing standards.
  • Co-designing what could be — a founder meeting in Barcelona in July 2025 shaped the Coalition's purpose, scope and initial programme of work.
  • Formalising the transition — an Interim Council meeting in London in December 2025 endorsed the governance model, membership approach and identity, and supported the move to independence.

We're opening membership and launching publicly in mid-2026, backed by our first wave of guidance and projects for 2026–2028.
