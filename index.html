Building a future where sand and silicate resources are sourced, used, and managed responsibly.

Sand and silicates are essential to modern life, yet largely invisible in discussions about responsibility, risk, and governance. The Coalition strengthens and accelerates responsible sourcing, use and production practices for these vital materials across sectors.

What we do

  • Surface and share evidence on sand and silicates risks and opportunities
  • Support alignment across standards and initiatives
  • Build a community of practice across sectors
