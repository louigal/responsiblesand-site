Building a future where sand and silicate resources are sourced, used, and managed responsibly.

Sand and silicates are essential to modern life, yet largely invisible in discussions about responsibility, risk, and governance. The Coalition brings actors together to strengthen and accelerate responsible sourcing and production practices across sectors.

What we do

  • Surface and share evidence on sand and silicates
  • Support alignment across standards and initiatives
  • Accelerate responsible sourcing and production practices
  • Build a community of practice across sectors
