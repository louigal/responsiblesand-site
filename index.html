Coalition for Responsible Sand and Silicates

Making the world’s most used materials visible — and responsible.

Sand and silicates are essential to modern life, yet largely invisible in discussions about responsibility, risk, and governance.

What we do

  • Surface and share evidence on sand and silicates
  • Support alignment across standards and initiatives
  • Accelerate responsible sourcing and production practices
  • Build a community of practice across sectors
